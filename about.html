We started out small!


Ride for Life started with only the founders of the app and 2 drivers! We have since expanded into a full 24/7, 100+ team.


We take pride in being a part of the community!


We have made sure that no matter what size our organization is, we keep connections to our community by giving back via donations and other services.


We are specialized for pregnancy emergencies!


Our team has been thorougly trained to be able to provide the safest, best transportation service for mothers in need.



Donation

Are you a mother in need of assistance but are struggling with income? Please contact our office to set up an arrangement with Drive for Life.

Our first priority is to make sure all our residents get the care they need. Please do not hesitate to ask for help!

Community

Not only do our drivers help save lives, but they also help with community services in their free time!

We do everything from Habitat for Humanity, food drives, to community cleanups. If you have a suggestion, please get in contact with us!
