What is Ride for Life?

Ride for Life is a group of dedicated individuals that provide safe, reliable transportation for in-labor mothers from their location to a nearby medical facility. We take pride in making sure that each interaction is expedient, while also accomodating for the most comfortable ride possible.

Responsive

We are right there when you need us! We know how important it is to have 24/7 access to medical transportation.

Safe

Our top priority is to get you safely to your destination! Our drivers are all safety trained to meet our promise to you.

Easy to Use

Our app is accesible to anyone with a smart phone, so you can access our assistance no matter where you may be!
